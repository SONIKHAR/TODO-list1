Welcome to Our Todo Website

Welcome to our todo website! We understand the importance of staying organized and productive, and that's why we created this platform to help you manage your tasks effectively.

How to Use

Using our todo website is simple and intuitive. Here's a quick guide to get started:

  1. Create a new task by typing in the task description in the input field.
  2. Click on the "Add" button to add the task to your list.
  3. To mark a task as completed, simply click on the checkbox next to it.
  4. To delete a task, click on the "Delete" button next to it.
  5. Feel free to edit any task by clicking on it and modifying the text.
